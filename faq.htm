AeroDoodle FAQ

AeroDoodle doesn't work, or looks strange

The only browsers currently supported are Chrome and Firefox

AeroDoodle runs really slowly

AeroDoodle relies on your computers graphics chip to compute the flow solution. The performance of AeroDoodle cannot be guaranteed due to the wide variety of graphics chips in use today

I drew a super accurate NACA airfoil, yet the lift and drag coefficients are different to those computed with other CFD software

AeroDoodle uses an algorithm that provides qualitative analysis only, use of AeroDoodle for the design of real components is not recommended.
