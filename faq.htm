WebTunnel FAQ

WebTunnel doesn't work, or looks strange

The only browsers currently supported are Chrome and Firefox

WebTunnel runs really slowly

WebTunnel relies on your computers graphics chip to compute the flow solution. The performance of WebTunnel cannot be guaranteed due to the wide variety of graphics chips in use today

I drew a super accurate NACA airfoil, yet the lift and drag coefficients are different to those computed with other CFD software

WebTunnel uses an algorithm that provides qualitative analysis only, use of WebTunnel for the design of real components is not recommended.
